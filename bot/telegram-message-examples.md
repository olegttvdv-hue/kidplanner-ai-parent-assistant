
---

## Parent Question Example

```text
My child does not want to go to bed on time. What can I do?
```

---

## AI Answer Example

```text
Try creating a predictable evening routine: quiet play, bath, short story, lights out at the same time every day.

Avoid screens before bedtime and keep the tone calm. The goal is not to force sleep immediately, but to make bedtime predictable and safe.
```
